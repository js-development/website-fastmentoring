Our Approach

Our staff are trained academic coaches that work individually with students to implement successful study techniques and habits that cater to each student’s specific needs and style. We work with students in a variety of subjects throughout all grade levels ranging from elementary school to graduate level courses.

Partner With Parents

We listen to parents, develop a strategy, implement that plan in our tutoring session and then send an email after each session to parents describing the session. This process keeps everyone informed and connected to each other.

Empowering Potential

FAST Mentoring provides academic tutoring in a wide range of subjects, including most A.P. and college level courses, all customized for your student’s learning style. Our tutors are current college students or recent graduates who demonstrate excellence in their areas of expertise, customize the session based on your student’s needs, teach difficult concepts in a relatable way, and find working with students to reach their full potential to be rewarding. We believe the foundation for long-term academic success comes from developing a student’s internal desire and self-confidence to work hard and avoid procrastination. That’s what makes us different than most “tutoring” programs. We don’t focus solely on the repetition and rote of the subject matter. Instead, we give students the tools and the confidence to empower their own academic journey.

Our two office locations, 4161 Steve Reynolds Blvd. near I-85 and 4729 Peachtree Industrial Blvd. near the Forum, provide us the opportunity to work with students attending public and private schools in Gwinnett and Fulton, homeschool students, as well as the option to meet virtually with collegiate students.
